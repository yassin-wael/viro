Little Nightmares II is a horror-adventure game that follows Mono, a boy who tries to escape a distorted world. He teams up with Six, a girl in a yellow raincoat, who helps him overcome the dangers of the Signal Tower, a source of evil. The game combines puzzles and platforming with stealth and survival. The game has a unique art style that contrasts the innocence of the characters and the darkness of their environment. The game is a sequel to Little Nightmares, but it can be played independently. The game explores themes such as identity, friendship, and freedom, and it delivers a captivating story.
